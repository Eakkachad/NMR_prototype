Based on the provided images, the proposal is for the **BDI YOUNG INNOVATOR HACKATHON 2026**. The template is divided into two structured pages. Page 1 focuses on the **"What" and "Why"** (Problem, Solution, Impact), while Page 2 focuses on the **"How"** (Technology, Workflow, Feasibility, and Evaluation Metrics).

Here is the breakdown of the required structure and content for this 2-page proposal document:

---

### Page 1: The Vision & Impact (Left Page)
This page establishes the foundation of your project. It must clearly define the problem, your target audience, your proposed high-level solution, and the concrete goals you aim to achieve.

**1. Header & Basic Information**
*   **Track Selection:** Choose the designated track (Track 1: Phenome, Track 2: Health, or Track 3: Smart City).
*   **Project Name/Title:** A clear, concise name for your project.
*   **Main Idea/Slogan:** A catchy, meaningful short phrase that encapsulates the project's core value.
*   **Target Audience:** Identify the specific groups of people who will benefit from your solution.

**2. Problem Statement (โจทย์ปัญหา)**
*   **Requirement:** Articulate the core challenge or pain point. Why is this issue significant? What is the current gap or limitation?

**3. Solution Approach (แนวทางการแก้ปัญหา)**
*   **Requirement:** Provide a high-level summary of your proposed solution. Describe how your approach will address the problem identified above.

**4. Expected Impact & Outcomes (ผลกระทบที่คาดหวัง)**
*   **Impact During Hackathon:** What can you realistically achieve and demonstrate within the Hackathon timeframe?
*   **Long-Term Impact (12-24 months):** What sustained change or social/business impact do you foresee after the Hackathon ends?
*   **Success Metric:** *Very Important.* Provide quantitative or clear qualitative metrics to measure success (e.g., "Reduce time by 25%", "Reach 10,000 users", "Improve efficiency by X").

---

### Page 2: The Execution & Evaluation (Right Page)
This page focuses on the technical implementation, logic, and scoring. Judges use this page to assess how realistic and innovative your plan is.

**1. Technology & Tech Stack (เทคโนโลยีและเทคโนโลยีที่จะใช้)**
*   **Technologies/Tools:** List the specific AI/ML models, databases, data pipelines (e.g., ETL), or development tools you plan to use.
*   **Platform & Deployment:** Specify how the solution will be delivered (e.g., Web app, Mobile app, Dashboard, API). Include details on security protocols (e.g., Secure data, Encryption, GATK) and deployment environments (e.g., Cloud, Docker, AWS EC2).

**2. Workflow Diagram (แผนภาพการทำงาน)**
*   **Requirement:** You must include a visual flowchart or schematic diagram illustrating how your project works step-by-step, from data input to the final user interface.

**3. Innovation & Feasibility (นวัตกรรมและความเป็นไปได้)**
*   **Innovation:** Explain what makes your solution novel or superior to existing alternatives (e.g., "Combines X and Y technology uniquely").
*   **Feasibility:** Explain why your solution is actually possible to build, including the technical approach and potential risks (e.g., "Leverages existing open-source libraries...").

**4. Evaluation Criteria / Self-Assessment (เกณฑ์การประเมิน)**
*This section is crucial as it directly links to how judges will score you.* You are expected to fill out the "คะแนน" (Score) and "โปรเจกต์ของผมตอบโจทย์อย่างไร" (How my project addresses this criterion) columns to self-evaluate against the rubric:
*   **Feasibility (30 pts):** Can you build a Prototype/PoC within 2 months? Explain your team's technical capacity.
*   **Problem Match & Dataset (25 pts):** Does your project perfectly match the problem statement? How will you source or handle the required Dataset?
*   **Sustainable Impact (20 pts):** How will your project create long-term, equitable change?
*   **Novelty (15 pts):** Why is your solution "New" and not just a copy of existing work?
*   **Implementation Plan (10 pts):** Does your team have a concrete plan to execute this within the hackathon timeframe?

**Total Score:** 100 Points.
